"The love of Beauty is Taste.
The creation of Beauty is Art."

— Ralph Waldo Emerson

As a designer I believe in creating functional, durable, and beautiful Places. Places are the spaces that you remember; that you make part of your life. Today, the spaces that we surround ourselves with are seldom memorable or meaningful. This should not be the case. We ought to fill our world with Places that are vivid and striking and significant.

Patricia O'Connor holds a Master of Architecture degree from the University of Notre Dame and Bachelors degrees in Classics and Mathematics from Gustavus Adolphus College. In addition to drafting and drinking coffee, Patricia enjoys singing, traveling, and cooking.
